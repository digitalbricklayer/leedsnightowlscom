# Road to Dunkirk Part Une: The German Blitzkrieg

## Overview

This introductory scenario represents the initial German breakthrough in the Ardennes, where British forces are caught off guard and must hold out against an unexpected German advance.

## Forces

Attacker (German) - 1000 points – Early War German

Defender (British) - 1000 points – Early War British

## Objective

The German player must move at least 50% of their units off the British deployment zone within six turns. The British player must prevent this.

## Deployment

Meeting engagement.

British - Begin with their commander and one infantry section deployed. Remaining units arrive from reserves starting on Turn 2.

Germans - Entire force enters the board on Turn 1.

## Board Setup

Place a road running diagonally across the board, with fields, woods, and a single farmhouse for cover.
