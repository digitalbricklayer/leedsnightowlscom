Games Played at Night Owls

Quite a wide range of games are played at the club. The club does not dictate what games members are able to play.

The Games

The following games have been played at the club over the last few months just to give you an idea of the games that are typically played. If a game is not in the list below, that does not mean that the game cannot be played. It just means that it hasn't been played. Again, the club is not at all prescriptive of the games people play.

This is not an exhaustive list, but covers the most popular ones.

Bolt Action

Bolt Action is a very popular World War 2 skirmish game and has been a regular at the club for many years with most members being familiar with the rules.

Flames of War

Another popular World War 2 ruleset at the club. Its popularity waxes and wains but it has a reasonably good following at the club.
