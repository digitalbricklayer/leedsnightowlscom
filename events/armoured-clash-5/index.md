# Bolt Action Armoured Clash 5

A multi-player game of Bolt Action that will focus on large scale armoured combat set during World War 2. The game will be held on 7th June 2026 during a [regular Leeds Night Owls club meeting](/meetings/).

The game is open to all members of the Leeds Night Owls club.

## Army Composition
Each player must build an 800-point list.

You are not required to include a rifle platoon for your list to be legal; however, any platoon you do include must comply with the platoon selector rules outlined in the Bolt Action rulebook.

## Order Dice

Each player will be limited to eight order dice.

## Terrain

Building – Standard bolt action rules

Trees – Hard cover, tree template blocks line of sight, troops, tracked, and wheeled vehicles cannot move through them

Hedges – Soft cover, troops, tracked, and wheeled vehicles can advance over them

Wall – Hard cover, Troops and tracked vehicles can advance over them

Road - Standard bolt action rules

## Timetable

10:00 – 10:15: Table setup.

10:15 – 10:45: Your lists will be presented to your opponents. You will be assigned separate-colored tokens for each of your units. You will discuss tactics with your teammates.

10:45 – 13:45: The game will commence.

## Housekeeping
Please do not leave it to the last minute to sign up for the game, and make sure you have your list written down when you arrive. Doing this will ensure minimum disruption before the game and will mean we will be able to get the game completed on time.

## The Battle Scenario
Seek and Destroy.
