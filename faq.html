Frequently Asked Questions

If you have any questions, please get in touch.

The club meets every Sunday between 10am and 2pm.

The club meets at The Cardigan Centre on Cardigan Road in Leeds. For more details about where we meet, please see the meeting page.

A lot of different games are played at the club. For a reasonably extensive list, please visit the games page for more information.
