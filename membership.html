Leeds Night Owls Membership

Night Owls is a membership organisation and charges a yearly membership fee and weekly fees used to pay for insurance at the venue and for rent on the room at The Cardigan Centre. Any surpluses are ploughed back into the club. You need to pay a modest yearly membership fee if you wish to attend. Also, each time you attend a weekly meeting there is a small fee to pay. The various fees are outlined in the table below.

Fee Cost
Weekly Fee for Waged £6
Weekly Fee for Retired / Unwaged £5
First Weekly Visit Free

Tuck Shop

There is a tuck shop provided each week for members. There is tea and coffee making facilities including continuous hot water from a hot water boiler available throughout each meeting. The tuck shop has a number of mugs available for members to use. You are responsible for cleaning your mug in the on-site kitchen at the end of the meeting. The tuck shop is run on an honesty box basis. The price list for the tuck shop is outlined below.

Product Cost
Tea & Coffee £0.50
Soft Drink Cans £0.50
Chocolate Bars £0.50
Biscuits £0.25
